>> hexadecimal is a pretty neat way to represent binary number.

Perform the following number conversions:
A. 0x39A7F8 to binary

3 = 0011
9 = 1001
A = 1010
7 = 0111
F = 1111
8 = 1000

0x39A7F8 = 001110011010011111111000

B. Binary 1100100101111011 to hexadecimal

1100 = 12 = C
1001 =      9
0111 =      7
1011 = 11 = B

1100100101111011 = 0xC97B

C. 0xD5E4C to binary

D = 13 = 1101
5      = 0101
E = 14 = 1110
4      = 0100
C = 12 = 1100

0xD5E4C = 11010101111001001100

D. Binary 1001101110011110110101 to hexadecimal

0010 = 2
0110 = 6
1110 = 14 = E
0111 = 7
1011 = 11 = B
0101 = 5

1001101110011110110101 = 0x26E7B5
